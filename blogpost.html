Welcome to the official website of the city of Zagreb! My name is Karlo and I am a 22 year old graduate student living in Copenhagen, Denmark. Since August of 2020 I have gained great interest in front end development after being a part of an IT start-up company for 3 months. I have asked my fellow co-workers what's the best way to gain skills for entering the inustry and they've recommended Udacity, so here I am!

The main goal of this blog is to present to others the city where I grew up in - Zagreb. I am originally Croatian and have moved to Denmark after finishing high-school. This is my fourth year living in Copenhagen and I hope to land a position as a front end developer some time in the future!

Feel free to contact me on LinkedIn if you'd like to know more about the city or if you're in the mood for a chat.
